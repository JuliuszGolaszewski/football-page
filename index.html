Now, if you don't know rules of football here are rules: Football matches are played between two teams and typically last for 90 minutes. The match is divided into two halves of 45 minutes each. There's a 15-minute break called half-time which separates the two halves. A match kicks off from the centre circle with one team in possession of the ball. The teams switch sides after half-time, with the other team in possession and kicking off proceedings.
